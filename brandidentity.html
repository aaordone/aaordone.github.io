brand identity

token vault

Token Vault is a brand identity and guide created for a mobile banking company, built around a randomized combination of letters and numbers. Using Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop, I developed a modern logo, color palette, and overall visual aesthetic that embodies innovation and creativity. The brand guide outlines core values, visual elements, and tone of voice, ensuring a consistent and professional identity across platforms, resulting in a cohesive brand that communicates creativity, sophistication, and cutting-edge design.

app & brand identity

refinery

Refinery is a mobile app and brand identity for a luxury beauty company designed to help consumers reduce their makeup consumption through personalized recommendations and product refill options. Designed in Figma with supporting visuals built in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, the brand and app experience blend convenience with sustainability, empowering users to make informed decisions, reduce waste, and invest in high-quality, long-lasting products, all within a sleek and sophisticated visual identity.

packaging & rebrand

cerave

CeraVe is a rebranding project focused on elevating an existing product's visual identity and market presence. Using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign, I modernized the packaging, typography, and visual language to better compete within its industry, highlighting the product's unique qualities while appealing to a more refined, contemporary audience. The redesign positions the product as a premium option, setting it apart from competitors and enhancing its shelf appeal.
