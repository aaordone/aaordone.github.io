360 marketing campaign

marcus graham project x phoenix suns

As part of the iCR8™ immersive sports marketing workshop through the Marcus Graham Project, our team took first place pitching live to Phoenix Suns executives. Working in partnership with the Phoenix Suns Phoenix Mercury Foundation, I helped lead creative direction and design execution for a strategic campaign engaging Phoenix youth and community culture around the 2027 NBA All-Star Game. Through research and a cohesive visual narrative, we translated community insights into a campaign the client could see themselves reflected in.

art direction toolkit

Reference

My process began with a brand audit of the NBA All-Star Game's existing visual identity to establish a creative direction rooted in consistency. I studied the game's current design language, including color systems, typography, and visual tone, to understand the established framework before introducing anything new. The objective was to develop a campaign that felt like a natural extension of the brand while carving out a distinct visual identity, ensuring the client could integrate the work seamlessly into their existing ecosystem.

Campaign Color Palette

The color palette was informed by the Phoenix Suns' existing brand system, but deliberately shifted to a distinct set of primary colors to create intentional separation between the team's identity and the All-Star Game campaign. The goal was to develop a palette that still felt rooted in Phoenix, evoking the energy and character of the city, while establishing a fresh event-specific visual identity that could stand independently from the Suns' current brand.

Environmental Imagery

Environmental imagery was sourced from the Sonoran Desert landscape, featuring sweeping mountain ranges and iconic saguaro cacti, to ground the campaign in a distinctly Phoenix sense of place. The natural terrain of the Southwest served as a visual anchor, reinforcing the campaign's connection to the city's identity while adding texture and depth to the overall aesthetic.

Graphic Elements

The campaign's graphic language was built around three core elements: star shapes nodding to the All-Star Game itself, a solid interpretation of the Phoenix Suns' iconic sunburst, and a basketball court outline used as a structural motif. Together these elements created a visual system that felt immediately tied to both the event and the city, giving the campaign a recognizable graphic identity that could be applied consistently across all deliverables.

Gradient Treatments

Two gradient systems were developed to carry the campaign's color narrative. The first draws directly from the warm tones of a Phoenix sunset, layering oranges and yellows to evoke the city's signature golden hour and ground the campaign in a sense of place. The second shifts into a cooler palette of purples and grays with subtle orange undertones, creating a bridge between the event's distinct visual identity and the Phoenix Suns' established brand colors. Together the two gradients gave the campaign visual versatility while maintaining cohesion across all applications.

exhibition design

museo nacional de antropología

Museo Nacional de Antropología: Honoring Life and Memory is a conceptual exhibit design created for the National Museum of Anthropology in Mexico City, centered on the cultural traditions of Día de los Muertos. Working as part of a four-person team, I was responsible for the entry and first half of the exhibit, designing the signage, marketing materials, and deliverables that set the tone for the entire visitor experience. Together we created a spatial narrative that honored the celebration while making its history and contemporary relevance accessible to a diverse audience.

brand collaboration campaign

phoenix mercury x glow recipe

Phoenix Mercury x Glow Recipe is a self-directed campaign concept pairing the WNBA's Phoenix Mercury with skincare brand Glow Recipe around the shared themes of confidence, wellness, and empowerment. Working solo from concept to execution, I developed a campaign system that balanced the bold, sporty energy of professional basketball with the soft, playful identity of a self-care brand, translating the concept across out-of-home, print, and social formats.
