ux design

college bound

College Bound is a single-page website designed to support first-generation Latino college students with accessible, culturally relevant guidance for navigating higher education. The project began with qualitative research into the goals and pain points of this community, which shaped wireframes and prototypes refined through multiple feedback loops. Built in Figma and developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the final site highlights college planning steps, scholarship resources, and motivational content through an interface designed for clarity, accessibility, and emotional resonance.

website design

crystal mocktails

A single-page website designed from scratch in Figma for a self-conceived functional beverage brand. Informed by a competitive analysis of brands like Kin Euphoric's and Hiyo, the design draws from the soft purples and muted lavenders common in the tonic space while developing a distinct visual identity for Crystal Tonics. The layout guides the user through a clear narrative flow from brand introduction to product exploration, social proof, and a final call to action.
