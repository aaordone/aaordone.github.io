motion design

american airlines

American Airlines is a motion design project that transforms a static poster into a dynamic animation, using Adobe After Effects, Illustrator, and Photoshop to bring key elements to life through movement, sound, and visual effects. The goal was to preserve the poster's original aesthetic while enhancing its storytelling, resulting in an immersive, visually engaging piece that amplifies the message and captivates the audience.
